## 4. Document setup  
## Page settings  
Use:  
* US Letter  
* Portrait orientation  
* 0.7-inch top and bottom margins  
* 0.75-inch left and right margins  
* Header distance: 0.3 inch  
* Footer distance: 0.3 inch  
* Vertical alignment: Top  
## Landscape pages  
Use landscape orientation only when it materially improves readability, such as:  
* Wide platform-comparison tables  
* Large screenshot sequences  
* Landscape iPad interface maps  
* Side-by-side portrait and landscape comparisons  
* Wide file-sharing or settings comparisons  
Do not rotate the entire guide to match the iPad screen.  
## Page numbering  
Recommended:  
* Cover: No visible page number  
* Page 2 onward: Arabic numbering  
* One continuous sequence  
* Automatic Word page-number fields  
## Section breaks  
Use only:  
1. After the cover  
2. Before and after landscape pages  
3. When a divider requires a different header  
4. Before back matter when necessary  
Limit section breaks to reduce footer, numbering and PDF-tagging problems.

## 33. Replacement and archive process  
When a replacement is approved:  
1. Publish the new PDF.  
2. Update SharePoint links.  
3. Update related QRGs and videos.  
4. Remove the previous version from active navigation.  
5. Change its lifecycle status.  
6. Move the Word source and PDF to Archive.  
7. Preserve version history.  
8. Add a replacement link when required.  
9. Remove active search keywords from the retired item.  
10. Remove retired AI retrieval tags from active knowledge sets.  
11. Confirm the new version is promoted.  
The governance standards require archived assets to remain available for history while being hidden from active learner views and active search promotion.
