## Video 1 — Confirm the account and mailbox  
## Learning goal  
Confirm the correct Outlook account and mailbox before beginning work.  
## Scene sequence  
1. Open Outlook.  
2. Open the account menu.  
3. Confirm the company account.  
4. Select the primary mailbox.  
5. Switch to the shared mailbox.  
6. Return to the primary mailbox.  
7. Show the account, mailbox and folder checks.  
## End card  
Confirm: App → Account → Mailbox → Folder
