## Video 8 — Draft and review with Copilot  
## Learning goal  
Create a clear prompt and complete the human review.  
## Prompt shown  
Draft a warm professional reply asking for the missing effective date. Keep it under 100 words and do not add new facts.  
## Scene sequence  
1. Start a reply.  
2. Open Copilot.  
3. Enter the prompt.  
4. Generate.  
5. Review the draft.  
6. Adjust or regenerate.  
7. Keep the draft.  
8. Edit manually.  
9. Confirm account, From address, recipients, facts and tone.  
10. Send.  
## End card  
**Copilot starts the draft. You finish the message.**
